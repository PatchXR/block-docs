## Description

6 dB/oct (one-pole) low-pass filter.

## Inputs, Outputs and Parts

**Signal input** *(stream input)*: The input signal.

**Cutoff frequency** *(stream input)*: Cutoff frequency of the filter (in Hz). Must not be less than one.
