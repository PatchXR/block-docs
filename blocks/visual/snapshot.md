## Description

Can be used to make 2D or 360 images, to display in the game.

Connected to image blocks, it can render live video or image for visual or use as texture.

## Inputs, Outputs and Parts

**grab**: 

**InteractiveSelector**: Connect this image block, to display what the snapshot sees.

**Render**: Render to connected image blocks.

**Save**: Render, save the image in your library, and create an image block with your image.

**Field of view**: Field of view in degrees.

**Projection mode**: 0: Classic, 1: 360, 2:Orthographic
