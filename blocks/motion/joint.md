## Description

Let you connect two physical parts together and control / constrain rotation. (let you make robotic arms, wheel, joystick etc)
- Place this joint inside a physical group.
- Connect the selector to a different group (or block).
Both group will be subject to the constraint.
Activate different features and degree of freedom from the Inspector.

## Inputs, Outputs and Parts

**Connected Part**: Connected block will become automatically physical if no "Make Physical" block is used. Can connect to only one block or group.

**Resize the shaft**: Only affect visuals.
