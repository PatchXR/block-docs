## Description

Add Force or Gravity to all blocks inside.

## Inputs, Outputs and Parts

**Resize**: Resize the area of the field.

**Strength**: Force strength.

**Only Include**: Will only apply force to connected blocks. (if no blocks connected, field will affect every blocks inside)

**Exclude**: Ignore blocks even if they are inside.

**Direction**: Direction of the force to apply.

**Is Global**: Will affets all physical blocks in the world.

**Is Global**: Will affets all physical blocks in the world.
