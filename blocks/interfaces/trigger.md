## Description

Hit or click to trigger a number (default 1) from the Jolt output.

Cand be used as a simple interface button, or a junction point to trigger multiple parts of a patch.

More behavior settings such as output number are available to change in inspector.

## Inputs, Outputs and Parts

**Trig Out**: Sends 1 when triggered (unless value changed in inspector).

Click & pull to generate a new Jolt wire.

**Trigger**: Triggers and sends 1 (default) when receiving any number.
