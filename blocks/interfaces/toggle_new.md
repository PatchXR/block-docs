## Description

Hit or click inside to change off/on state, sending 0 or 1 by default from the Jolt output.


Can be edited and used in many ways to switch states of other blocks.

## Inputs, Outputs and Parts

**State out**: Sends 0-1 (by default) depending on the toggle state. Click inside the cube to switch the outputed number.

Click & pull to generate a new Jolt wire.

**Set state**: Send a jolt message to change the on/off state of the toggle.
By default, any number will flip current state. Check this block's Inspector to change its input and output behavior.

To connect another block here, click & pull on any Jolt output to create a wire and drag it here.
