# Bubble Popper

**Category**: 🎮 Controllers

![bubble_popper thumbnail](https://portal.patchxr.io/block-thumbnails/bubble_popper.png)

## Description

An underwater creature used to delete sample bubbles. Grab and bring a bubble close to its spikes to pop it.

Convenient way to clean up sample bubbles created by Bubble Maker, Bubble Recorder or Microphone in EP1 Fluid Echoes. Simply grab a bubble and move it onto the spikes to make it pop and disappear.
