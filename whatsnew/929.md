> Hotfix follow-up to the **Direct Fork Audio Connections** change in 77.3.1.

### 🔌 Fork & Connection Fixes
- **Restored audio through nested forks:** Fixed a regression where a stream routed through a nested fork (a fork feeding another fork — for example a cross-scope output tap on an instrument) could carry **no signal** when patched live. The signal now reaches its destination immediately, without needing to save and reload the scene.
- **Accurate fork readouts:** A freshly created fork tap now reports its live value right away on hover/inspect, instead of showing `0` until it was reconnected.
