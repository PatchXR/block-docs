## 🚀 PatchWorld Update: Version 77.1.12 900

### 🎨 Creative Tools & UI
*   **Rich Text Support:** The **Text Block** just got a major brain upgrade. It now supports Rich Text tags (via TextMeshPro), meaning you can finally stop using ten different blocks for one sentence.
    *   *What this means:* You can now mix colors, **bold**, *italics*, or different font sizes within a single text block.
    *   [Check the official tag guide here](https://docs.unity3d.com/Packages/com.unity.textmeshpro@4.0/manual/RichText.html).
*   **Web Browser Interaction:** New Inspector settings allow you to choose exactly what a "click and drag" does on a web page. Switch between **Scrolling** through content or **Moving items** within the site.

### 🎥 Desktop & Camera
*   **Third-Person Mode:** Desktop players can now attach the camera to their player model in third-person. 
    *   *Pro tip:* This is perfect for checking out your own avatar's style or capturing more cinematic gameplay footage without the "shaky cam" feel.

### 🛠️ Interaction & Workflow (QoL)
*   **Smarter Knobs:** We’ve made it much easier to turn small knobs when they are covered in wires. 
    *   *The Logic:* If your wires are set to "hidden" and you hover over a knob, the system will now ignore the invisible wire hitboxes. No more accidentally grabbing a cable when you just wanted to tweak the volume!

### 🐞 System & Stability
*   **PCVR Copy-Paste Fix:** Fixed a frustrating bug where copying text from Windows into PatchWorld would fail to save. 
    *   *The Culprit:* Windows uses a hidden "carriage return" character that was breaking our save files. We now clean that up automatically so your text stays right where you put it.
