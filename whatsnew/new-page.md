## PatchWorld Update: Version 77.1.9 898

This update streamlines our multiplayer systems, polishes the "Ghost" experience, and ensures your creative tools behave exactly as expected. Here’s the organized breakdown of what’s improved.

---

### 🌐 Multiplayer & Social
*Keeping the connection stable and the vibes right.*

* **World Syncing:** Fixed a critical issue where the global world state could break if the "Master" player logged off during a sync. The handoff is now much smoother.
* **Voice Chat Upgrades:** * Switched to a more reliable voice system to squash microphone glitches in multiplayer.
    * Added a **Voice Chat Volume** slider to the settings!
* **Cleaner UI:**
    * Invites and "Request to Join" notifications no longer appear during loading, but one the world is loaded.
    * Fixed a visual glitch affecting how player names are displayed above avatars.

---

### 👻 Ghosts & Recording
*Improving your digital echoes.*

* **Reliable Ghosts:** Fixed an issue where "Ghosts" (recorded sessions) would become broken or uninteractive if their files hadn't fully loaded.
* **Ghost Audio:** Added a **Volume Slider** directly into the Ghost Inspector, giving you more control over your recordings.
* **Say Cheese: Desktop Screenshot** button and the **Snapshot block**
    * Screenshots now correctly render wires.
    * Disabled the headset recording preview for desktop users to keep things tidy.

---

### 🧱 Blocks & Interactions
*Refining the tools of the trade.*

* **Device & Controller Blocks:** Removed the visual "stick" on the controller for **device_handle** and **controller_output** blocks for a cleaner look.
* **Drumming Physics:** Prevented the "wand tip" from stretching annoyingly when you're drumming on hittable blocks. Jam out with confidence.
* **Boards Optimization:** Adjusted the **Level of Detail (LOD)** distance for Toggle & Sliderboard blocks.
* **Avatar Fixes:** Fixed a bug that caused players to become frozen in place after swapping their avatar. You are now free to change outfits and keep moving.
