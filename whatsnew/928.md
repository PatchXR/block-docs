### 🤖 AI Agent & MCP Integration
- Integrated the new external and in-app AI patching agent, enabling automated scene building and curation.
  - See [In-App AI Agent](/en/patching/agent)
  - See [Connecting Patchworld MCP to AI Agents](/en/patching/mcp)


### ⚡ Group & Fork Connectivity Enhancements
- **Direct Fork Audio Connections:** Optimized forks by routing them directly rather than through intermediate audio engine nodes, reducing overhead and audio latency.
- **Robust Group Rewiring:**
  * Fixed a bug that disconnected forks when grouping or ungrouping.
  * When grouping blocks, if any block is connected to an external `fork_input` that is not part of the selection, the system now automatically includes the `fork_input` inside the group to preserve the connection.
  * Ensures spawned forks are correctly created inside the group they belong to, maintaining proper hierarchy.
  * Allowed spawning items from outside a group in multiplayer sessions when a player initiates a fork from the outside.

### 🛠️ Controller & Input Improvements
- **Restored HTC Vive Wand Support:** Revived wand support for HTC Vive controllers.
- **Valve Index Tuning:** Increased the grip threshold sensitivity on Valve Index controllers to prevent accidental grab actions.
- **Automated PSVR2 Setup:** Added auto-configuration routines to automatically apply recommended settings for PSVR2.
- **Separated Trackpad & Thumbstick Controls:** Split trackpad and thumbstick behaviors into separate action mappings, governed by player settings.
- **Ignore Touchpad Toggle:** Added a new setting under **Settings > Inputs** allowing players to completely ignore touchpad/trackpad inputs (useful for avoiding accidental touches or addressing hardware drift).
