# 🚀 What’s New: The VRM Update
**Special to our beloved players ❤️ for their feature requests and bug reporting!**

---

### 🎭 Avatars & Personalization
* **Custom VRM Avatars:** The wait is over! You can now bring your own VRM avatars into Patchworld.
* **Avatar Management:** Quickly reset to the original look using the new **"RevertToDefaultAvatar"** console command, or find the option directly in the **Profile** and **Library** menus.
* **Ghost Recording Fix:** You can now resize your player while recording a "Ghost"—playback will now correctly reflect your scale changes.

### 🛠️ Building & Tool Enhancements
* **Expanded Geometry:** New shapes added to the **Shape Block**, including Hexagon, Pentagon, Octagon, Curved Screen, and Fisheye Disk.
* **Advanced Text Editing:** All blocks with text inputs now feature an **"Edit From Outside The Group"** option, making it much easier to manage complex patches.
* **Clean Workspace:** The "Hide Inputs/Outputs" toggle now also hides **Tag and Selector** I/Os for a truly clutter-free view.
* **Smart Saving:** Cutting a bubble (via Radial Menu) no longer automatically saves the newly recorded bubble to your assets, keeping your library clean.

### 🌐 Multiplayer & Social
* **Voice & Lip-Sync Fix:** Fixed a bug where the "talking indicator" and lip-sync animations would break after a player unmuted themselves.
* **Audio Stability:** Implemented an experimental fix to reduce random audio glitches during multiplayer voice chat sessions.
* **Optimization:** Improved performance for player name displays and controller syncing in multi-user environments.

### 🕹️ UI & Experience
* **Natural Scrolling:** Click-and-drag scrolling is now enabled for the **Library** and **Web View**. This works seamlessly with both controllers and **Hand Tracking**.
* **Visual Fidelity:** A new **"Details Factor"** slider is available in *Settings -> Graphics*. You can also trigger this via the `SetDetailsFactor` command to dynamically change the level of detail in your current world.

### 🖥️ Desktop Mode
* **Recording Quality:** Prevented microphone saturation (clipping) when recording video on the Desktop version.
* **Visual Fixes:** Resolved an issue causing "white spots" on specific shaders in Desktop mode.
