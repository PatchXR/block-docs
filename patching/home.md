# Patching Hub

Patchworld is a modular creative sandbox where you can build interactive environments, games, and audio engines using a system of **Blocks**. This page serves as a central hub for all patching-related documentation.


## 🚀 Fundamentals
Everything you need to know to start building in Patchworld.

- **[Blocks](/blocks)**: The core units of Patchworld. Prefabs with logic and interactive parts.
- **[Library](/patching/library)**: Browse and spawn blocks, devices, and community assets.
- **[Connections](/patching/connections)**: Connect blocks using **Jolts** (events), **Streams** (audio), and **Tags** (references).
- **[Inspector](/patching/inspector)**: Fine-tune block settings, colors, and behavior.
- **[Groups & Devices](/patching/groups-and-devices)**: Bundle blocks together into reusable, sharable modules.
- **[Play vs Edit Mode](/patching/play-vs-edit-mode)**: Switch between building and experiencing your world.
- **[Console Commands](/patching/console-commands)**: Advanced tools for debugging and automation.

## 🔊 Audio & Sound Design
Create complex synthesizers and soundscapes.

- **[Audio Filters](/patching/audio-filters)**: Shape your sound with EQs, filters, and effects.
- **[Samples](/patching/samples)**: Record, play, and manipulate audio samples.

## 🧠 Logic & Data
Manage state and create dynamic behaviors.

- **[Variable System](/patching/variable-system)**: Use global and local variables to store information.
- **[Online Variables](/patching/online-variables)**: Sync data between players in multiplayer sessions.
- **[Preset Manager](/patching/preset-manager)**: Save and recall the state of your blocks.
- **[Dynamic Patching](/patching/dynamic-patching)**: Modify your patch logic programmatically at runtime.

## 🎮 Interaction & Input
Connect the user and the outside world to your patch.

- **[Player Inputs](/patching/player-inputs)**: Map controller buttons, triggers, and gestures to actions.
- **[External Connections](/patching/external-connections)**: Connect via OSC or other protocols to external software/hardware.
- **[Mixed Reality (MR/AR)](/patching/mr-ar)**: Integrate your patches with the physical world.

## 🏗️ Physics & Mechanics
Build moving parts and physical simulations.

- **[Physics](/patching/physics)**: Gravity, collisions, and physical forces.
- **[Joint System](/patching/joint-system)**: Link blocks with hinges, springs, and fixed joints.
