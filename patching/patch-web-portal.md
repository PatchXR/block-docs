This page explains what you can do on the Patch Portal.

portal.patchxr.io

## What this portal is
- **Purpose**: Browse and manage PatchXR content on the web, upload your own assets, generate new assets with AI, manage your Ṽibz wallet, and discover worlds and creators.


## Key capabilities
- **Browse library content (no login required)**
  - Discover featured assets, worlds, and creators through `discover`, `community` tabs
  - Main public library is the `+Create` tab 

- **Manage your personal assets (login required)**
  - `Upload` section. 
  - Upload via drag-and-drop and file picker
  - You can also see your library in the `+Create` tab (upload and create tab to be merged soon)
  
- **Generate AI assets (login required; consumes Ṽibz)**
  - Skybox generation
  - Image-to-3D mesh
  - Text-to-audio

- **Manage Ṽibz wallet and purchase bundles (login required)**
  - View coin balance, my transactions, and buy Ṽibz bundles.
  - Purchases handled via Xsolla (VS Meta on the headset)

- **Worlds and patches**
  - Social actions include likes and share links.
  - You can like worlds, this will add worlds to your likes, for later easy find.
  - Editing permissions and meta data is possible as well 


## Asset uploads and types
- **Supported types**: `.wav`, `.ogg`, `.mp4`, `.avi`, `.mov`, `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.gif`, `.glb`, `.patch`
- **Limit**: 100 MB per file (enforced in the UI; server may enforce stricter limits).
- **Playback/previews**: Images show thumbnails; samples can be played inline; models and other types integrate with custom viewers where available.
